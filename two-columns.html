Our Brand

Vision

In a world where it is necessary for expats to learn Dutch. We believe that by providing interactive posts & relatable scenarios we are able to help them interact more in ths new environment. Because only then they will build confidence to get out there and socialize more. 

Contact

We would love to get in contact with you and help you with questions or suggestions! You can contact us through Facebook and Instagram.

Values

Inclusivity

We want to make sure that everyone feels welcome in this new environment. As a company we make sure you're welcome, valued and respected, no matter who you are or where you come from.

Communication

In order to make things work, communication is always imporant. That's why we prioritise communication in our online database and through our webinars/events. All to make the learning process the most satisfying for you.

Support

Support is something that is crucial for you as an expat. We will make sure that we always are open to help you and guide you through the whole process of learning a new language.

Commitment

In order to really make this work, a certain level of commitment is needed. From you, and from us. We will make sure that you will be motivated to work on this learning phase. This is going to be a fun experience for both sides!
